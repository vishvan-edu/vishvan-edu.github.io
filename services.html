Our Services

Classes 6th to 8th

We provide a strong foundation for younger students in subjects such as Mathematics, Science, and English. Our interactive approach helps students develop problem-solving skills and prepares them for future academic challenges, including competitive exams like Olympiads.

Classes 9th to 10th

We focus on preparing students for their board exams and competitive exams. Our curriculum is designed to ensure a deep understanding of core subjects, such as Maths, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), and English, while emphasizing problem-solving and analytical skills.

Classes 11th to 12th

We offer specialized coaching for students in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. For Science students, we focus on JEE and NEET preparation, ensuring in-depth knowledge of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology. For Commerce and Arts students, we offer focused coaching for board exams and help in subjects like Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Humanities.

JEE Preparation

Our JEE coaching program is designed to provide students with expert guidance in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. We focus on building strong fundamentals, practice, and time management skills, ensuring success in both JEE Mains and JEE Advanced exams.

NEET Preparation

Our NEET coaching helps students develop a thorough understanding of Biology, Physics, and Chemistry. We emphasize problem-solving, analytical thinking, and MCQ practice to help students excel in the NEET exam for admission to medical colleges.

UP Board Preparation

We provide comprehensive coaching for UP Board students, focusing on exam-specific strategies, subject mastery, and performance optimization to help students achieve high marks in their board exams. Our expert teachers help in all subjects according to the UP Board syllabus.

CBSE Preparation

We offer focused coaching for students appearing for CBSE board exams, ensuring a deep understanding of subjects like Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Science. Our preparation methods are aligned with the CBSE syllabus, helping students secure excellent grades.

ICSE Preparation

For ICSE board students, we provide expert coaching in all subjects, focusing on detailed understanding and application of concepts. Our teaching methods are designed to help students excel in their ICSE exams while developing critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

About Us

Vishvan Educational Society is a leading institute in UP, providing quality education for students from classes 6th to 12th. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge and skills to achieve their dreams.
