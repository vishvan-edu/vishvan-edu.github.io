Our Courses

JEE (Joint Entrance Examination)

Our JEE coaching provides a comprehensive understanding of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. We help students master key topics such as Mechanics, Organic Chemistry, and Calculus. Our specially designed test series, mock exams, and personalized guidance ensure a high success rate in both JEE Mains and JEE Advanced.

  • Physics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Waves, Optics
  • Chemistry: Physical Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry
  • Mathematics: Algebra, Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, Probability

NEET (National Eligibility Entrance Test)

Our NEET preparation program is designed to give students an in-depth understanding of Biology, Physics, and Chemistry. We focus on important topics like Human Physiology, Organic Chemistry, and Electrostatics. Students are trained to tackle the NEET pattern with precision through rigorous practice and expert guidance.

  • Biology: Human Anatomy, Physiology, Genetics, Ecology
  • Chemistry: Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry
  • Physics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Optics, Electrodynamics

CBSE Boards (Classes 10th & 12th)

We provide focused coaching for CBSE students in subjects such as Mathematics, Science, and Social Science. Our courses are designed to build a deep conceptual understanding, helping students excel in their board exams. We also focus on strengthening writing skills and exam-taking strategies.

  • Mathematics: Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Calculus
  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Social Science: History, Geography, Political Science, Economics

UP Boards (Classes 10th & 12th)

Our UP Board courses focus on subjects according to the UP Board syllabus. We offer coaching for all major subjects, helping students develop a strong foundation for their board exams. Our approach combines conceptual learning with exam-centric practice sessions.

  • Mathematics: Algebra, Calculus, Geometry, Statistics
  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • English: Grammar, Literature, Writing Skills

ICSE Boards (Classes 10th & 12th)

Our ICSE Board preparation program is designed to ensure that students are well-versed in the intricacies of the ICSE syllabus. We offer expert coaching for all subjects, focusing on detailed understanding, problem-solving, and application-based learning to help students perform at their best in the exams.

  • Mathematics: Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, Statistics
  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • English: Literature, Writing Skills, Comprehension
